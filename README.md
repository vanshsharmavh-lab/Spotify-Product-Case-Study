# Spotify Adaptive Daily Mix - Product Management Case Study

## Overview

This project is a Product Management case study focused on improving Spotify's music discovery experience.

The goal was to identify a user problem, understand user behavior, prioritize solutions, and design a product strategy that improves engagement and retention.

---

## Problem Statement

Spotify's recommendation system is one of its strongest features, but over time some users may feel that recommendations become repetitive.

When users repeatedly hear similar songs and artists, music discovery can become less exciting, reducing engagement and the feeling of a personalized experience.

### Key Question:

How might Spotify help users discover fresh music while maintaining personalized recommendations?

---

# User Research

## Target Users

### Students and Young Users

- Use Spotify frequently for studying, travelling, relaxing, and entertainment.
- Want quick access to music that matches their mood and activity.

### Working Professionals

- Use Spotify during commute and work.
- Prefer convenient and personalized listening experiences.

---

## Research Questions

1. How often do you use Spotify?
2. How do you usually discover new music?
3. How satisfied are you with Spotify recommendations?
4. Does your music preference change based on mood or activity?
5. If you could improve one thing about Spotify, what would it be?

---

# Key Insights

Based on user observations and research hypotheses:

- Users enjoy personalized recommendations but want more variety.
- Users want new music discovery without losing familiar songs.
- Searching manually for music creates friction.
- Music choice changes depending on mood and activity.

---

# Proposed Solution

## Adaptive Daily Mix

Instead of replacing Daily Mix, improve it with two recommendation experiences.

## 1. For You 🎧

A personalized listening mode focused on familiarity.

Includes:

- Favorite artists
- Similar songs
- Preferred genres
- Frequently played music

Goal:

Provide comfortable and reliable recommendations.

---

## 2. Explore 🌍

A discovery-focused mode.

Includes:

- New artists
- Trending songs
- Different genres
- Music liked by users with similar tastes

Goal:

Help users discover fresh music.

---

# User Feedback System

After listening, users can provide quick feedback:

😍 Loved it

🙂 Good

😐 Okay

🙁 Not for me


Additional feedback:

- More like this
- More variety
- Less like this

This helps Spotify continuously improve recommendations.

---

# Prioritization

Using the RICE framework:

| Feature | Priority |
|---|---|
| Improve Daily Mix | High |
| Mood-based playlists | Medium |
| AI Music Assistant | Future |
| Social Sharing | Lower |

## Decision

Improve Daily Mix first because:

- Existing user familiarity
- Lower development risk
- Faster validation
- High potential impact

# Final Recommendation

Spotify should evolve from a recommendation system into a personalized music companion.

By balancing familiar music with discovery, Spotify can create a more engaging experience while improving long-term user retention.
